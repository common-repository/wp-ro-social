Automatically add links on your posts to social bookmarking sites/[Ro]Adauga automat linkuri spre site-urile de social bookmarking sub fiecare post.

== Description ==
Based on [Sociable](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/sociable/) WP RoSocial automatically add links to romanian social bookmarking sites on your posts or pages. You can choose from a list of 18 websites/tools.
[Ro] Bazat pe Sociable, WP RoSocial adaugat automat linkuri spre cele mai cunoscute site-uri de tipul social bookmarking romanesti in fiecare post sau pagina de pe blogul tau (in functie de setari).

== Installation ==

**Install**
1.Download and unzip `wp-rosocial.zip` file.
2.Upload `WP RoSocial` folder with its files to your `wp-contents/plugins` folder. 
3.Activate it from Wordpress Administration -> Plugin Page

**[RO]Instalare**
1.Descarca dupa care dezarhiveaza `wp-rosocial.zip`.
2.Incarca folderul `WP RoSocial` impreuna cu continutul sau in folderul `wp-contents/plugins`.
3.Activarea se face din Panoul de administratie -> Plugins
